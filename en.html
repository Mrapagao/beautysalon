Our Services

Lymphatic Drainage

Massage therapy aims to stimulate and improve the lymphatic and immune system, eliminating toxins such as fat nodules and cellulite. Its benefits include prevention and elimination of cellulite and localised fat, relief from tired and blotchy legs due to fluid retention, post-operative treatment, improvement in acne, rosacea, migraines, arthrosis, arteritis and more. Schedule your session now and experience the benefits of lymphatic dernage.

Reflexology

It works the whole organism, stimulating the refluxes of the internal organs, glands and parts of the body, as well as massaging its exterior. All the organs of our body are represented on the soles of our feet. By applying pressure to specific points on the feet we are stimulating these organs sending waves of relaxation throughout the body causing a general well-being, preventing illnesses, alleviating pain, contributing to a better quality of life.

Geothermal Massage

This massage originated in the Orient approximately two thousand years ago. It has been used throughout the ages by various civilizations for therapeutic purposes. It is a massage that acts essentially to relieve pain and stress, rebalancing and increasing the vital energy of the body. Harmonises body, mind and soul!

Therapeutic Massage

Also known as massage therapy, massage consists of the application of force or vibration on soft tissues in order to stimulate blood circulation, mobility, elasticity and relief of body pain. Schedule your session now and experience the benefits of massage therapy.

Kobido

An ancient massage created in Japan in the middle of the 15th century, it was one of the royalty's favourite wellness rituals, especially for emperors. This massage is composed of a set of draining, vigorous and relaxing movements, activating local circulation, improving the oxygenation of the cells and the distribution of nutrients between them, giving the skin back its tone. It is possible to notice an improvement in the contour of the face oval, more defined and toned from the very first session.

Amazonian Massage

A relatively recent massage imported from the tropical regions of South America, inspired by the movement of the hammocks and habits of the indigenous peoples' expressions of affection. Delicacy and movement are the basis of this massage, it has a calming, relaxing and stabilising effect.
